About Us

Generally..

We are the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars. We are a globally recognized motorsport, known for its high speeds, intense competition, and technological advancements. Teams compete in a series of Grand Prix races around the world, with championships awarded to both drivers and constructors (teams).
